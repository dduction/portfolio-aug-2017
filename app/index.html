About

Franklin Farahani

I’m a web developer based in Vancouver, BC with a focus on creating beautiful and intuitive UI.

I discovered my passion for web development as soon as I finished building my first website. My love of the web prompted me to attend the Computer Science and New Media and Web Development programs at Simon Fraser University and BCIT, respectively.
